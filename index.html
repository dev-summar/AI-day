PROJECTS TO SHOWCASE...

I. High-Impact Citizen Services (AIKosh & User-Defined)

VakilSetu (LegalTech)

A voice-based GPT that classifies legal cases (Criminal/Civil) in regional languages and matches users with rated advocates for introductory (₹500/15min) or emergency assistance calls.

ScamRakshak (Cyber-Security)

A mobile app detecting phishing messages and "Digital Arrest" calls. Features sender verification, bank legitimacy checks, and a self-improving local AI model to prevent economic losses.

SabhaSaar (Gram Sabha AI)

Automates the generation of structured minutes and action points from audio/video recordings of village panchayat meetings in 14 Indian languages.

Kisan Call Centre Assistant

Uses transcripts from the national Kisan Call Centre (AIKosh dataset) to provide instant, offline-capable voice advisories to farmers regarding pests and crops.

Virtual Negotiation Assistant

An AI tool for MSMEs and individuals to resolve disputes via automated document analysis, outcome prediction, and assisted drafting of settlements.

Intelligent Agent Mapping

AI-driven tools that automate the onboarding and matching of vendors (MSMEs) to government tenders based on their operational capabilities.

COILD Tourism Companion

A regional travel assistant using the COILD parallel corpus (AIKosh) to provide high-quality translation and cultural insights for tourists in 18+ Indian languages.

Vedic Literature Translator

Uses the Sanskrit-Interlingua-Hindi-Kannada dataset (AIKosh) to translate and provide style-insights into ancient Indian texts for modern readers.

Geospatial Mineral Mapper

Integrates AI with GSI geological maps (Karnataka/Andhra datasets) to predict mineralization zones and assist in mineral exploration.

IndicNER Healthcare

Uses the IndicNER model (11 languages) to extract critical medical entities from regional language prescriptions and medical notes to digitize rural health records.

II. Operational MIET Projects (Refined)

Seedo.AI (Smart Automation)

Automates attendance and vehicle tracking (ANPR, overloading) on campus, integrated with mParivahan for safety monitoring.

Caretaker.ai (Healthcare)

A portable monitoring solution for the elderly that tracks vitals and analyzes behavior patterns to alert caregivers proactively.

Crickeye (Sports)

An AI cricket coach that uses vision-based analysis to provide feedback on a player's performance and posture.

PetPulse (Pet Care)

A full life-cycle platform for pet health, licensing, and trade management using RFID, Blockchain, and PetGPT.

Sky Netra Simulator

A defense-tech simulation for Aad interceptors, optimizing debris fall-away zones in urban areas.

Smart Apple Sorter

A computer-vision machine for regional farmers to sort apples by quality and size.

Pi360 Academic Analytics

A data engineering framework for institutional quality tracking and student performance analytics.

Smart Bridge Monitor

Uses sensor arrays to predict the structural integrity of bridges based on real-time vibration data.

Classroom Teaching Effectiveness

A CV system that measures student attentiveness (privacy-preserving) to help faculty improve delivery.

TLC GPT

A custom LLM trained on institutional data to assist new faculty in generating curriculum-aligned content.

Saarthi.ai (AI Counsillior)

An AI counsellor platform designed to provide guided emotional support, student wellness check-ins, and timely referral pathways for professional help when needed.

Asra Seva

A disaster relief management platform for disasters and calamities. It uses LoRa modules for low-connectivity areas and smart bands for victims and relief supervisors to coordinate, track movement toward shelters, and monitor health vitals effectively.

III. Healthcare & Life Sciences

OSCC Oral Cancer Screening

Uses clinical and radiological datasets (AIKosh) to detect early-stage oral cancer from smartphone images.

Veda-Fit (Yoga Guru)

An AI assistant that checks weight distribution and breathing patterns (via audio ML) for 50+ Yoga asanas.

Sleep Apnea Acoustic Detection

Analyzes nocturnal breathing sounds to flag potential sleep apnea issues before clinical testing.

SAMARTH (Mental Health)

An AI screening tool for adolescents to detect neuromotor and mental health issues early.

TB Audio-Screening

A low-cost tool that uses cough-sound analysis to provide early TB screening in rural areas.

IV. Cybersecurity & Emerging Tech

Predictive Zero-Trust Auditor

Streams system states into a time-series model to flag unauthorized process mappings before data breaches.

Autonomous Multi-Agent Bug Hunter

A swarm of AI agents that pulls code from GitHub, scans for vulnerabilities, and suggests automated fixes.

Deepfake Voice Detector

Protects against voice-cloning scams by analyzing spectral inconsistencies in audio.

Blockchain Science (DeSci)

A decentralized platform for peer-reviewing research data using Smart Contracts to ensure reproducibility.

Sound-wave Offline Payments

Enables UPI-like transactions in remote areas without internet via encrypted high-frequency sound waves.

V. Agri-Tech & Sustainability

BhuPRAHARI

Monitoring rural water bodies (Amrit Sarovars) using geospatial AI and satellite imagery.

AI-Powered Crop Insurance

Automates crop damage assessment using satellite data for faster farmer payouts.

Forest Fire Early Warning

A hybrid system using IoT sensors and satellite data to predict Himalayan forest fires.

Carbon-Aware Compute Scheduler

A "Green AI" tool that shifts heavy compute tasks to times when the energy grid is using renewable sources.

Smart Solar Load Balancer

Forecasts energy consumption in micro-grids to optimize solar battery distribution.

VI. Smart Infrastructure & Education (New Suggestions)

Pothole Detection Agent

Uses smartphone sensors on local vehicles to automatically map and report road damage.

Zero-Knowledge E-Voting

A privacy-preserving voting system for campus elections using ZK-proofs.

AR Technical Assistant

An AR app that provides step-by-step repair instructions for electronic devices using computer vision.

Socratic STEM Tutor

A multi-lingual AI tutor that teaches through questioning rather than just giving answers.

AI-Proctored Decentralized Exams

A secure system for remote exams using eye-tracking and environmental audio analysis.

Crowd Lens Campus

Real-time safety monitoring for campus gatherings to detect violence or density anomalies.

Building Violation Monitor

Analyzes satellite imagery to detect unauthorized construction in restricted zones.

E-Waste Circular Marketplace

Categorizes e-waste components using CV to facilitate recycling and reuse.

Digital Twin for Urban Floods

Simulates city drainage during rains to optimize emergency response.

Kirana Forecasting Agent

Predicts inventory needs for local shops based on seasonal trends and local events.

AI Music Composer (Indic)

Blends modern beats with classical Indian Ragas for student film scoring.

Skill-Graph Career Path

Generates dynamic skill roadmaps for students based on real-time industry trends and grades.

Sign Language to Speech

A mobile app translating regional Sign Language gestures into spoken Hindi/English.

Industrial IoT Maintenance

A predictive pipeline that uses sensor data to foresee industrial machine failures.

MSME Tender Matchmaker

AI agent that maps small business capabilities to public sector tenders automatically
